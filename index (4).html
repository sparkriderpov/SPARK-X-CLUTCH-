◆ HUD LAYOUT ◆

OPTIMIZE YOUR HUD

WHAT IS HUD?

HUD (Heads-Up Display) refers to the button layout on your screen during gameplay. A well-configured HUD directly impacts your reaction speed, aim control, and overall combat performance.

Position your fire button where your thumb rests naturally for instant trigger response. Keep scope and crouch buttons within comfortable reach to enable smooth drag-headshots.

Place the fire button at the right edge — lower thumb position = faster drag shots.
Keep scope & aim buttons on the left side for independent control.
Use 3-finger or 4-finger claw grip for maximum movement + aim control.
Reduce HUD button size slightly to get a wider field of view.
